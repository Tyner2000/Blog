The start of it all,  
is a beautiful things.  
The first colors of fall,  
or a glistening ring.


Rebirth comes next  
following loss.  
A cross to bear,  
but at what cost.


Death is said to be final,  
but is that a curse,  
or a gift to be had,  
for better or worse.
